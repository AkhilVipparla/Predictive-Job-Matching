
We are seeking a skilled Data Analyst to interpret data, analyze results, and provide insights to support business decision-making. The ideal candidate will have a strong analytical mindset, proficiency in data manipulation and visualization tools, and the ability to communicate findings effectively to stakeholders. The Data Analyst will work closely with cross-functional teams to gather and analyze data, identify trends and patterns, and translate data into actionable recommendations that drive business growth and operational efficiency.

Responsibilities:

Collect, clean, and preprocess data from various sources to ensure accuracy and consistency.
Perform exploratory data analysis to identify trends, patterns, and relationships in the data.
Develop and maintain reports, dashboards, and visualizations to communicate key insights and metrics to stakeholders.
Conduct statistical analysis and hypothesis testing to validate findings and make data-driven recommendations.
Collaborate with business stakeholders to define key performance indicators (KPIs) and metrics to track business performance.
Identify opportunities for process improvement and optimization based on data analysis.
Proactively monitor data quality and integrity, and implement corrective measures as needed.
Stay up-to-date with the latest trends and technologies in data analysis, and advocate for their adoption as appropriate.
Communicate findings and insights to technical and non-technical audiences through written reports, presentations, and data visualizations.
Qualifications:

Bachelor's degree in mathematics, statistics, computer science, economics, or a related field (or equivalent work experience).
Proven experience as a Data Analyst or similar role, with proficiency in data analysis and visualization tools such as SQL, Python, R, Excel, Tableau, or Power BI.
Strong analytical and problem-solving skills, with the ability to derive insights from complex datasets.
Familiarity with statistical methods and techniques for data analysis, such as regression analysis, clustering, and time series analysis.
Experience with data cleaning, preprocessing, and transformation techniques.
Excellent communication and presentation skills, with the ability to communicate technical concepts to non-technical stakeholders.
Attention to detail and accuracy, with a commitment to delivering high-quality work on time.
Ability to work independently and in a team environment, with a proactive and collaborative approach to problem-solving.
