We are seeking a skilled AWS Developer to design, implement, and maintain cloud-based solutions on the Amazon Web Services (AWS) platform. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in software development and experience with AWS services to build scalable, secure, and reliable applications. The AWS Developer will work closely with cross-functional teams to understand business requirements, design architecture, and deploy solutions that leverage AWS cloud technologies.

Responsibilities:

Design, develop, and deploy cloud-native applications and microservices on AWS.
Implement and manage AWS services such as EC2, S3, Lambda, API Gateway, DynamoDB, and RDS.
Architect scalable and resilient solutions that meet performance, security, and compliance requirements.
Develop automation scripts and infrastructure as code using AWS CloudFormation, Terraform, or similar tools.
Optimize application performance and cost efficiency by leveraging AWS best practices and services.
Ensure high availability, fault tolerance, and disaster recovery capabilities in AWS deployments.
Monitor and troubleshoot AWS resources, services, and infrastructure to identify and resolve issues.
Collaborate with DevOps engineers, software developers, and other stakeholders to streamline CI/CD pipelines and deployment processes.
Stay up-to-date with the latest AWS services, features, and best practices, and advocate for their adoption as appropriate.
Qualifications:

Bachelor's degree in computer science, engineering, or a related field (or equivalent work experience).
Proven experience in software development, with a focus on cloud-based solutions.
Strong understanding of AWS cloud architecture, services, and features.
Proficiency in at least one programming language such as Python, Java, or Node.js.
Hands-on experience with containerization and orchestration technologies like Docker and Kubernetes is a plus.
Familiarity with DevOps practices and tools for continuous integration and continuous deployment (CI/CD).
Excellent problem-solving and troubleshooting skills.
Strong communication and collaboration skills, with the ability to work effectively in a team environment.
AWS certifications such as AWS Certified Developer, AWS Certified Solutions Architect, or AWS Certified DevOps Engineer are a plus.
